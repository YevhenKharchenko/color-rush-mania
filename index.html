About the Game

Color Rush Mania is a vibrant and addictive puzzle game that challenges your logic, speed, and creativity. Move colorful boxes through matching gates, fill them with balls, and pack them into the dock before it overflows! Use special skills like Sort and Rainbow Gate to stay ahead, unlock new challenges with keys, and beat the ticking Ice timer.

Whether you're a strategic mastermind or a casual puzzle fan, Color Rush Mania brings fast-paced fun, brain-teasing levels, and a splash of color to every move.

How to Play

  • 1

    Move boxes in the direction shown by the arrow.

  • 2

    Match box and gate colors to pass through.

  • 3

    Fill boxes with colored balls before sending them to the dock.

  • 4

    Keep an eye on the Ice timer — it will shatter when it hits zero!

  • 5

    Collect matching keys to unlock locked boxes.

  • 6

    Use skills like “Sort” and “Rainbow Gate” to solve tricky situations.

Skills and Power-ups

  • Use "Sort" to clear the dock area.

  • "Rainbow Gate" sends stuck boxes away.
